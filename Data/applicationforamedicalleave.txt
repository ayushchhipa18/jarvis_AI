Here is a sample application for medical leave:

**Application for Medical Leave**

**To,**
[Name of Head of Department/Manager]
[Department/Company Name]
[Date]

**Subject: Application for Medical Leave**

Respected [Head of Department/Manager's Name],

I am writing to request a medical leave of absence from [Date of Leave] to [Expected Date of Return]. Unfortunately, I am currently unwell and am undergoing medical treatment for [briefly mention the nature of illness/injury].

Due to my health condition, I am unable to attend work and require some time to recover and recuperate. I have attached a medical certificate from my doctor, which provides more details about my condition and the expected duration of my leave.

I apologize for any inconvenience this may cause and will do my best to catch up on any missed work as soon as I am able to return to the office. I will keep you updated on my progress and provide any necessary documentation to support my leave.

I appreciate your understanding and approval of my request. If there is any additional information required, please do not hesitate to contact me.

Thank you for your support during this time.

Sincerely,

[Your Name]
[Your Employee ID/Designation]

**Medical Certificate (attach a copy)**

Note: Please modify the above application as per your specific needs and requirements.
